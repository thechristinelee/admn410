Financing

We accept Visa, Mastercard, AmericanExpress, and Discover cards. Cash is accepted in stores, and we also accept PayPal online.

Delivery

When purchased before 5 PM EST M-F, we will ship the product(s) via USPS that evening. Anything purchased after 5 PM EST will go out the following day. We provide tracking to the email we send confirmation, and packages are shipped Priority Mail and should arrive between 2-5 business days.

Return Policies

WE DO NOT ACCEPT RETURNS ON OUR PETS. THIS IS A NON-NEGOTIABLE.

In the case that you are unsatisfied with a product with the exception of petfood, we will honor an exchange or refund for the full amount within 14 days of purchase with a receipt. If you do not have the receipt, we will exchange or refund the current price which may have been discounted since the date of purchase. If you purchased online, you may return for store credit or a full refund amount with the return slip we provide in our shipped boxes. If you need another box, you may find free boxes provided by USPS here.
